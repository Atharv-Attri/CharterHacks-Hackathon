The Problem

Contact tracing today asks for much more information than is actually necessary. As a result less people use it, and lives that could have been saved are lost.

So we thought, can there be contact tracing that doesn't go into people's privacy?

The ideal contact tracing app would let users remove data they do not want the app to see, it would also allow them to disable tracking whenever they want. This app would also not ask for any information except for the person's location. Finally this app would not let any human see the person's data, the only thing interacting with the data would be a computer.

Well, we have the solution!
